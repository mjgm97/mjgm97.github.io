Manuel J. Gomez

Manuel J. Gomez is working towards a Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Murcia, Spain. He obtained his B.Sc. Degree with a focus on applied computing and data science, and a M.Sc. in Big Data. He is a member of the CyberDataLab in the University of Murcia, and his research interests include data mining, educational technology, game-based assessment, and natural language processing.
